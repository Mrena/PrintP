Printing Companies & Individuals

If you own an image printing company or you are an individual who own high quality image printing facilities, and your area is not covered by our service as yet. You can register with us to do print-outs and delivery to our clients. The business process is quite simple.


  1. When one of our client from an area you operate or can deliver to uploads a photo for printing, you will receive an email with the photo to be printed, required size of print-out, and the name, contact details, and address of the client.
  2. You print out and deliver the required number of print-outs.
  3. Our client pays you for the service rendered.
  4. At the end of the month you pay us 1% of the generated revenue through our clients.
